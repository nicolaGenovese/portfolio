About me

I am a UX/UI designer with 10+ years of experience in the field and I am currently open to new opportunities to lend my skills to helpful, engaging and innovative digital products. In my previous roles, I was described as a resilient, reliable, and altruistic natural leader. I have an inquisitive personality and I am a great listener as I love learning about how people think, what drives their motivation and why they act the way they do.

My constant need to improve myself pushes me to put my learnings into practice every day getting out of my comfort zone.

I consider myself a very creative person who finds inspiration from everyday things like music, art or all the small things I can observe in the streets during my commuting time.

I strongly believe in the power of simplicity and in the need to put customers in the centre of what we do. Therefore I am an advocate of design thinking and agile methodology.
